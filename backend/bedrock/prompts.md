# Bedrock Prompt Examples

## Security Finding Summary

Analyze this AWS security finding and explain the risk in simple language.

Include:
- what happened
- why it matters
- possible compliance impact
- recommended remediation

## HIPAA Impact Review

Review this finding from a healthcare compliance perspective.

Include:
- possible PHI or PII exposure
- access control concerns
- logging or encryption concerns
- recommended next steps

## Executive Summary

Summarize this finding for a non-technical manager.

Focus on:
- business risk
- priority
- action needed
